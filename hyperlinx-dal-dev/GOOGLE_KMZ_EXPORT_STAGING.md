# Google KMZ Export Staging

The Google Helium RFP requires vendor proposed KMZ spans.

This phase stages KMZ readiness only. It does not generate, write, or export KMZ files.

Future KMZ export should consume:

- Route requirement
- Centerline route
- Stationed corridor
- Proposed graph
- Customer naming rules
- Workbook span targets

KMZ staging must preserve customer-supplied KMZ evidence and must not overwrite customer geometry.
