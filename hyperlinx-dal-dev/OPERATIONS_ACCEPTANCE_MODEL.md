# Operations Acceptance Model

Status: doctrine and contract alignment.

## Acceptance Types

Operations Authority supports:

- Asset Acceptance.
- Facility Acceptance.
- Power Acceptance.
- Transport Acceptance.
- Network Acceptance.
- GPU Acceptance.
- Data Center Acceptance.
- Customer Acceptance.
- Operational Acceptance.
- Composite Acceptance.

## Acceptance Meaning

Acceptance remains evidence until Operations Authority validates readiness.

Acceptance does not:

- activate billing.
- recognize revenue.
- activate monitoring.
- create production service.
- modify Field closure history.
- modify Completion history.

## Acceptance Requirements

An acceptance record must identify:

- acceptance ID.
- acceptance type.
- referenced assets, services, facilities, customer acceptance, or composite scope.
- evidence IDs.
- accepted status.
- accepted by.
- accepted at.

Operations cannot be approved without accepted operational readiness criteria.
