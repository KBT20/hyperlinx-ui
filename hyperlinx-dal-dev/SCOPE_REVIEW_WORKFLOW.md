# Scope Review Workflow

Phase: 6.8B

## Workflow

1. Translate prepares evidence.
2. ScopeVersion remains the review truth object.
3. Participants add comments.
4. Participants propose redlines.
5. Authorized stakeholders record review decisions.
6. Review status is evaluated.
7. Approved reviews may be marked ready for Prism.

## Review Status

- DRAFT
- UNDER_REVIEW
- REVISION_REQUESTED
- REVIEW_COMPLETE
- APPROVED_FOR_PRISM
- BLOCKED

## Handoff

Scope Review can indicate readiness for Prism, but it does not execute Prism scoring and does not create authority.
