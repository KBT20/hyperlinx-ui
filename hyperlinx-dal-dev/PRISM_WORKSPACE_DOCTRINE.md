# Prism Workspace Doctrine

Phase: 6.8I

## Core Rule

Prism advises. Humans decide. Engineering validates. Marketplace responds.

Prism identifies possibilities, risks, alternatives, and commercial signals. Prism does not create authority.

## Boundary

Prism may consume:

- Opportunity context
- Baseline Network candidates
- Scope Review status
- Marketplace assets
- Candidate sites
- Network affinity signals
- Route Engineering inputs

Prism may present:

- Opportunities
- Risks
- Alternatives
- Cost drivers
- Diversity gaps
- Marketplace matches
- Advisory recommendations

Prism may not:

- Mutate ScopeVersions
- Approve designs
- Create lifecycle authority
- Create commercial authority
- Persist execution state
- Call production services in this phase

## Workflow

Customer

Opportunity

Prism is the advisory optimization layer between customer collaboration and preliminary quote preparation.
