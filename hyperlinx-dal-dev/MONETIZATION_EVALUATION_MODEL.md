# Monetization Evaluation Model

Status: doctrine only.

## Objective

Monetization evaluation estimates residual corridor value after the primary customer requirement is satisfied.

Residual monetization is secondary but important. It must not override the required hyperscaler, neocloud, or transport objective.

## Residual Value Factors

Evaluate:

- spare ducts.
- spare fibers.
- available transport capacity.
- future productization.
- duct sale potential.
- dark fiber IRU potential.
- recurring transport opportunity.

## Opportunity Types

Potential residual opportunities include:

- enterprise.
- wireless tower.
- ISP.
- WISP.
- school.
- municipal.
- utility.
- data center.
- carrier.
- government.

## Product Mapping

| Product | Monetization signal |
| --- | --- |
| DUCT_SALE | spare ducts, buyer proximity, maintenance model |
| DUCT_MAINTENANCE | duct sale plus operations requirement |
| DARK_FIBER_IRU | spare fibers, route diversity, term |
| MANAGED_FIBER | operations capability and customer demand |
| WAVE_SERVICE | optical system capability and interconnect demand |
| ETHERNET_TRANSPORT | recurring service opportunity |
| AI_INTERCONNECT | hyperscaler/neocloud demand |
| ROUTE_OPERATIONS | maintenance and restoration service |

## Risk Controls

Monetization scoring should flag:

- speculative demand.
- unclear customer proximity.
- missing residual capacity.
- unresolved ownership.
- uncertain maintenance cost.

Monetization is recommendation support only. It is not authority.
