# Google RFP Response Validation

Validation scenarios:

## Helium to Muskogee

The route requirement creates a design launch session, stationed corridor, civil mix estimate, preliminary quote package, and Tab D vendor response preview.

## Helium to Stillwater

The route requirement creates the same outputs and carries a diversity dependency against Helium to Muskogee.

## Vendor Response

Tab D fields are staged as preview data only. Workbook writing is intentionally out of scope.

## Submission

The checklist tracks KMZ, workbook, quote, review, and submission readiness. No external submission occurs.
