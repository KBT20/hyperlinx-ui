# Google Civil Mix Quote Doctrine

Civil mix converts centerline corridor takeoff into budgetary construction categories:

- PLOW
- HDD_BORE
- OPEN_TRENCH
- BRIDGE_ATTACHMENT
- RAILROAD_CROSSING
- WATER_CROSSING
- URBAN_CONSTRUCTION
- UNKNOWN

Rural long-haul centerline segments bias toward plow. Crossings and unknown constraints bias toward HDD or engineered crossing allowances.

Cost precedent may come from prior Google Fiber and Dobson/Google ILA workbooks, but final pricing remains budgetary until engineering and commercial review.

Every civil mix output is labeled SALES_ESTIMATE and PENDING_ENGINEERING_VERIFICATION.
