# Preliminary Quote Confidence Model

Phase: 6.8J

## Confidence Values

- `LOW`
- `MEDIUM`
- `HIGH`

## Confidence Basis

Confidence is based on:

- Data completeness
- Marketplace completeness
- Architecture completeness
- Review completeness
- Prism completeness

Overall confidence is the lowest of the supporting confidence values.

## Interpretation

Low confidence means the quote should not be used for customer discussion without resolving blockers.

Medium confidence means a customer discussion may occur with explicit caveats.

High confidence means the preliminary quote is ready for customer discussion but remains non-contractual.

## Boundary

Confidence is advisory only and does not create commercial authority.
