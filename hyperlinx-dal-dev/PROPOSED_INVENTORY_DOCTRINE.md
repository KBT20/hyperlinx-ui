# Proposed Inventory Doctrine

Proposed Inventory is a sales and design orchestration estimate.

It is not an Inventory Graph.

It is not a ScopeVersion.

It is not engineering-certified truth.

## Doctrine

The Design engine creates Proposed Inventory.

Proposed Inventory supports Preliminary Quote.

Engineering certifies only after customer acceptance.

Sales estimates first. Engineering validates second.

## Boundary

Proposed Inventory may contain estimated mileage, segments, stations, crossings, vaults, fiber feet, duct feet, and construction type.

Proposed Inventory may not:

- create inventory records
- modify Inventory Graphs
- create ScopeVersions
- mutate geometry
- invoke routing
- invoke stationing
- authorize execution

## Sales Role

Proposed Inventory helps a salesperson explain the likely commercial scope before Route Engineering.
