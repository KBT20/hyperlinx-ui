# SOF Readiness Requirements

Status: doctrine and read-only requirement contract.

## Required Authority

SOF readiness requires:

- valid `scopeVersionId`.
- valid customer traceability.
- lifecycle state at or beyond `CUSTOMER_ACCEPTED`.
- validated `ENGINEERING_CLOSE`.
- validated `BUDGET_CLOSE`.
- validated `CUSTOMER_ACCEPTANCE_CLOSE`.
- validated vendor acceptance when vendor scope is applicable.

## Required References

SOF readiness verifies:

- customer.
- product plan.
- service description.
- service locations.
- approved capacity.
- term assumptions.
- pricing or budget references.
- engineering approval.
- customer acceptance.
- vendor acceptance when applicable.
- approved object package.

## Meaning

`SOF_READY` means the package has enough evidence to prepare a Service Order Form.

It does not mean contract execution occurred.

It does not create legal obligation.

It does not create execution authority.
