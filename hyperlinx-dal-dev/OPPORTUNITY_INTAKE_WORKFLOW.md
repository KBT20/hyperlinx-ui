# Opportunity Intake Workflow

Phase: 6.8A

## Workflow

1. Capture customer context.
2. Capture opportunity context.
3. Register attachments.
4. Register locations.
5. Register objectives.
6. Validate intake completeness.
7. Generate Opportunity Package Candidate.
8. Evaluate Translate readiness.
9. Translate prepares evidence.
10. Scope Review coordinates customer collaboration before Prism.

## Ready For Translate

An opportunity is ready for Translate when:

- Customer is present.
- Opportunity is present.
- At least one location is present.
- At least one objective is present.
- No critical blockers exist.

## Blocked

An opportunity is blocked when required customer, opportunity, location, or objective information is missing.

## Review Handoff

Opportunity Intake does not collaborate on ScopeVersion truth directly. After Translate prepares evidence, Scope Review becomes the customer collaboration layer before Prism.
