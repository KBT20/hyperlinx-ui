# Constitutional Authority Audit

Status: audit contract and validation report.

## Authority Boundaries

The authority audit verifies:

- Prism is advisory.
- Marketplace is advisory until commercial close authority.
- Budget lock is commercial truth, not execution.
- Control authorizes execution.
- Field performs execution.
- Completion authorizes completion.
- Operations authorizes operational readiness.
- Twin projects truth but does not create truth.
- Operational Intelligence observes portfolio state but does not mutate ScopeVersion truth.

## Audit Checks

The authority audit detects:

- authority leakage.
- authority overlap.
- authority bypass.
- invalid authority escalation.
- AI advisory actors performing authority-bearing actions.
- advisory-only layers creating execution authority.

## Diagnostics

Authority diagnostics include:

- `[AUTHORITY_BOUNDARY_VALIDATED]`
- `[AUTHORITY_VIOLATION]`

## Result

Authority passes only when no advisory-only system creates execution, completion, operations, or lifecycle authority.
