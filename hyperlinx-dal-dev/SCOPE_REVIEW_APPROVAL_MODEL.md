# Scope Review Approval Model

Phase: 6.8B

Review approvals capture stakeholder disposition.

## Decisions

- APPROVE
- APPROVE_WITH_COMMENTS
- REJECT
- REQUEST_REVISION

## Rule

Review approvals do not change lifecycle.

Review approvals do not create authority.

Review approvals do not create ScopeVersions.

Approval status may indicate readiness for Prism, but it does not execute Prism.
