# Parallel Runtime Risk Model

Phase: 6.7D

Parallel Runtime records risk for each comparison area.

## Risk Types

- Authority risk
- Lifecycle risk
- Traceability risk
- Marketplace risk
- Control risk
- Field risk
- Completion risk
- Operations risk
- Production cutover risk
- Audit risk

## Risk Levels

- `LOW`
- `MEDIUM`
- `HIGH`
- `CRITICAL`

## Risk Fields

Each risk includes:

- Risk type
- Level
- Component
- Message
- Production cutover impact

## Critical Path

Authority, lifecycle, and traceability risks are critical-path risks. High or critical risk in those categories blocks controlled adoption.
