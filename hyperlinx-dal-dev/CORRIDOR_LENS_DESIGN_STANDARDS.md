# Corridor Lens Design Standards

Lens-level standards define which object standards matter most under a commercial or strategic view.

The corridor does not change.

The lens changes which engineering questions rise to the top.

## HYPERSCALER

Hyperscaler corridors elevate:

- power proximity
- route diversity
- data center handoff
- regen and optical review
- high fiber count
- future conduit capacity
- security
- restoration

## NEOCLOUD

Neocloud corridors elevate:

- GPU array support
- high-capacity transport
- power
- carrier hotel access
- cloud access
- dark fiber or transport optionality

## DUCT_MONETIZATION

Duct monetization corridors elevate:

- spare duct accounting
- sale-eligible duct
- maintenance model
- ISP, WISP, and utility opportunity proximity

## DARK_FIBER_IRU

Dark fiber IRU corridors elevate:

- strand reservation
- route diversity
- splice points
- handoff design
- IRU boundary

## TRANSPORT

Transport corridors elevate:

- topology
- optical design
- regen placement
- ADM placement
- SLA
- restoration

## ENTERPRISE

Enterprise corridors elevate:

- laterals
- access construction
- service availability
- building entry
- commercial serviceability

## POWER_AI_EXPANSION

Power and AI expansion corridors elevate:

- substations
- transmission
- generation
- parcels
- future campus suitability
