# Google Route Diversity Doctrine

Route diversity is advisory in this phase.

The model can compare two route geometries and produce:

- Shared mileage estimate
- Shared corridor percentage
- Separation warning
- Diversity status

Statuses:

- NOT_EVALUATED
- LIKELY_DIVERSE
- POTENTIAL_OVERLAP
- NOT_DIVERSE
- REQUIRES_ENGINEERING_REVIEW

The Helium to Stillwater route carries a dependency requiring comparison against the Helium to Muskogee route.

Final diversity is a Route Engineering determination.
