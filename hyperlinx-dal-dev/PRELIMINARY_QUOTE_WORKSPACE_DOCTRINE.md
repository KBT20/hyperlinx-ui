# Preliminary Quote Workspace Doctrine

Phase: 6.8J

## Core Rule

Prism identifies opportunities. Marketplace identifies capabilities. Quote assembles a commercial recommendation.

Humans decide. Engineering validates. Contracts occur later.

## Quote Boundary

The Preliminary Quote is:

- Advisory
- Preliminary
- Non-contractual
- Engineering validation required

The Preliminary Quote does not create:

- Contractual authority
- Budget lock
- Contract
- Service Order Form
- ScopeVersion authority
- Execution authority

## Workflow Alignment

Opportunity
↓
Translate
↓
Scope Review
↓
Prism
↓
Preliminary Quote
↓
Marketplace
↓
Execution

Quote is the commercial decision-support layer before formal marketplace, contract, and execution workflows.
