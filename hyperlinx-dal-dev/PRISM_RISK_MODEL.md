# Prism Risk Model

Phase: 6.8I

## Risk Categories

Supported categories:

- `DIVERSITY_RISK`
- `COST_RISK`
- `CAPACITY_RISK`
- `POWER_RISK`
- `FACILITY_RISK`
- `CONSTRUCTION_RISK`
- `RIGHT_OF_WAY_RISK`
- `MARKETPLACE_RISK`

## Risk Fields

Each risk includes:

- `riskId`
- `riskCategory`
- `severity`
- `summary`
- `mitigation`
- `evidenceIds`

## Cost Drivers

Cost drivers are advisory summaries for:

- Construction
- Power
- Facility
- Marketplace
- Right of way
- Engineering

Cost drivers do not become budget locks, vendor awards, or execution authority.
