# Prism Advisory Card Model

Phase: 6.8I

## Advisory Categories

Supported categories:

- `ROUTE_OPPORTUNITY`
- `FACILITY_OPPORTUNITY`
- `MARKETPLACE_MATCH`
- `COST_REDUCTION`
- `DIVERSITY_IMPROVEMENT`
- `CAPACITY_IMPROVEMENT`
- `POWER_OPPORTUNITY`
- `GPU_OPPORTUNITY`
- `LAND_OPPORTUNITY`
- `RISK_ALERT`

## Card Fields

Each advisory card includes:

- `cardId`
- `category`
- `title`
- `summary`
- `confidence`
- `impactScore`
- `evidenceIds`
- `advisoryOnly`

## Authority

Advisory cards are observations. They do not approve designs, mutate geometry, create ScopeVersions, create quotes, or launch work.
