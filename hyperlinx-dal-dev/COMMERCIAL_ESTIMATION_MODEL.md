# Commercial Estimation Model

The commercial estimation model produces advisory commercial quantities and product estimates.

It uses assumptions only.

No engineering certification is performed.

## Quantity Categories

Supported advisory quantity categories:

- Conduit Feet
- Fiber Feet
- Fiber Count
- Regen Count
- ADM Count
- Laterals
- Crossings
- Data Center Interconnects
- Carrier Hotel Interconnects
- Cloud On-Ramps
- Power Reviews
- Permit Categories

## Confidence Inputs

Commercial confidence depends on:

- Evidence completeness
- Reference Architecture fit
- Design standard completeness
- Human review blockers
- Missing objects
- Missing tool evidence

Confidence values:

- LOW
- MEDIUM
- HIGH

## Risk Inputs

Commercial risks include:

- Missing power validation
- Route diversity not reviewed
- Jurisdiction uncertainty
- Crossing uncertainty
- Data center access uncertainty
- Interconnection uncertainty
- Optical review required
- Engineering review required
- Vendor pricing unavailable
- Budget not established

## Estimate Boundary

The model does not call pricing APIs.

The model does not integrate Marketplace.

The model does not create a quote of record.

The model does not create ScopeVersions.
